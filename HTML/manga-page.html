Who is Gege Akutami?

Gege Akutami is the pseudonymous manga artist behind the hugely popular dark fantasy series Jujutsu Kaisen. Despite his real identity being shrouded in secrecy, Akutami’s creative impact on the manga world has been seismic. Gege Akutami’s real name is not publicly known. The manga artist uses Gege Akutami as a pen name and has not revealed his actual identity. The popular manga artist Gege Akutami keeps his personal life very private. While his exact birth date is unknown, most sources believe he was born around 1992. This would make Akutami about 31 years old today in 2023. He was in his mid-20s when he first found success with his manga series Jujutsu Kaisen in 2018.
